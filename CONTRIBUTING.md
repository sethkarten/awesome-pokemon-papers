# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing to Awesome Pokémon Papers!

## Adding a Paper

1. Fork this repository.
2. Add your entry to `README.md` under the appropriate section.
3. Submit a pull request with a link to your paper.

### Paper Entry Format

```
- **Paper Title** — Authors (Year). Brief description.
  [[Paper]](https://link-to-paper) [[GitHub]](https://link-to-code)
```

## Verified Leaderboard Submission

To get a :white_check_mark: on a leaderboard, include verification materials in your PR:

### Battling Leaderboard
- Your Pokémon Showdown account username
- Elo screenshot or link to your account's rating
- Alternatively, a link to reproducible evaluation logs

### Speedrun Leaderboard
- Video recording or replay file of your agent completing Pokémon Emerald
- Final real-time

Unverified papers can still be listed in the papers section — verification is only needed for leaderboard placement.

## Quality Standards

- Papers must present original research on AI agents playing Pokémon.
- Prefer peer-reviewed or well-known preprint papers, but strong workshop papers are welcome.
- Include working links to the paper and code (if available).
